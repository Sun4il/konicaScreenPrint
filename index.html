About Us

Konica Screen Print is a professional screen printing company providing high-quality prints on apparel and merchandise. We are dedicated to delivering top-notch products with a focus on creativity and customer satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art equipment and skilled team ensure that every print is perfect, from single items to large-scale orders.
